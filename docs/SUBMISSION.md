## 1. Project Details
**Project Name:** FamileXYZ
**Track:** Theme 1: AI & Agents
**Challenge Statement:** Basic Problem Statement (AI & Agents) - Create a verifiable on-chain Agent

**Project Description (98 words):**
FamileXYZ is a privacy-first AI platform designed to strengthen family bonds through five specialized agents. Built on Hedera, it leverages the **HCS-10** standard to create a verifiable, on-chain "Family Log" of emotional milestones and shared wisdom. Our agents—Wisdom, Intimacy, GenerationalBridge, Presence, and Growth—act as proactive family coaches, not just chatbots. They use Hedera Consensus Service (HCS) to immutably record family achievements and the Hedera Token Service (HTS) to reward engagement with $FAM tokens, creating a transparent, autonomous economy of emotional wealth within the family unit.

## 2. Tech Stack

- **Frontend:** React, Vite, TailwindCSS
- **Backend:** Node.js, TypeScript
